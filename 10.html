Role Detail: Production Line Operator & Brickmaker

Work Context
Stands at a workbench, conveyor, press, or robot. Operates clay shaping machines, visually inspects products and line flow.

Constraints: Dirty hands and protective gloves. Cannot type on a keyboard (desktop is out of the question).
Key Tasks (User Stories)
  • Report machine fault / process error
  • Request leave / view shift schedule
  • Request shift swap with colleague
  • Log worked hours and breaks
  • Quick log of piece count (quota)
