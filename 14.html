Role Detail: Kiln & Dryer Operator

Work Context
Operates in high-temperature and high-noise environments. Monitors burners and firing curves. Requires immediate reaction times.

Equipment: Heat-resistant gloves, industrial tablet, or ruggedized mobile phone.
Key Needs (User Stories)

  • Log gas consumption directly at the machinery
  • Instant overheat alarms (Vibration/Audio alerts)
  • Report burner failure to maintenance with one click
