STARE Model Use Case: Foreman - Remote Attention Control

S
SITUATION: URGENT REQUEST FOR "AKU" BRICKS
During general cleaning, an urgent request for "AKU bricks" arrives. The deadline is critical. Summoning 20 people for a physical meeting would waste 15 minutes.
T
TASK: TEAM SYNCHRONIZATION WITH ZERO LATENCY
The foreman must immediately synchronize the team to the new priority without a physical meeting. The goal is to change team behavior with zero latency.
A
ACTION: FOREMAN CHANGES PRODUCTIVITY WEIGHT REMOTELY
Foreman on tablet: 1. "Line Changeover" weight 5 → 1 (Priority). 2. "Hall Cleaning" weight 1 → 7 (Postponed).
R
RESULT: REMOTE CONTROL OF ATTENTION
Icons are rearranged on all terminals. "Cleaning" disappears, and "Change Mold" appears. Saving 15 minutes of downtime.
E
EVOLUTION: MANUAL CONTROLLER AS A FOUNDATION FOR AI
A shift from a static model to a manual remote controller. The system learns priorities for a future AI autopilot.
