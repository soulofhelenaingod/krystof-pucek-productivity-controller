Security Strategy and Compliance

Identity & Authentication
Supports any identity provider. Passwords never leave the secure perimeter of your existing solution.
Google Cloud Identity | Keycloak | Microsoft Entra Identity
Standard: OpenID Connect and OAuth 2.1
Access Control
Permissions are defined by groups in your central directory. Differentiation of rights for data reading and production writing.
Methodology: Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
Production Data Protection
Mobile devices never communicate directly with the production network or machinery. The backend serves as an impenetrable security shield.
Network isolation and validation of all communication
Audit & Traceability
Every critical action is logged with an immutable timestamp and identity. Full readiness for corporate security audits.
Compliance with ISO 27001 Standards
