Phase One: Silent Gate and the End of Passwords

Passive Mode
Strategy & Psychology
Adoption through convenience. People naturally migrate to the app launcher because it is a faster path to work without constant password entry. Habit and trust building without production disruption.
Goal: Unifying entry and removing barriers
User Experience
Employees work the old way, but a new, subtle panel appears at the edge of the screen. Everyone now has a single icon on their tablet; opening it logs them in instantly.
Outcome: One entry, zero passwords
Minimum Viable Product (MVP)
Deployment of the client part on the Ionic platform and the server part in Java. Identity integration via OpenID Connect and implementation of Single Sign-On. A static list of all corporate applications.
Technology: SSO / Microsoft Entra / Google Identity
